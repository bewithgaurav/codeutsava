#RaipurIsBeautiful

Looks like it was just yesterday when I was bidding tearful adieu to my parents, scared, for being banished away from home for the next 4 years. #RaipurIsBeautiful ??No, No it’s not merely beautiful, it’s way more than that. It’s the city that transformed a girl to a lady, it’s the city that made me cry and laugh at the same time, it’s the city that taught me what it is to be independent, what it feels like to love unconditionally, to be brave, to raise voice against injustice, to dance like no one’s watching, to sing like no one’s hearing and to live like there’s no tomorrow. It’s the city where I am a friend, a sister, a junior, a senior, and a daughter all at once. It’s the city where I came as Ananya and I leave as just Annu.
No word can ever fully capture the beauty this city beholds. From the eyes of a tourist, it’s just like any other Indian city, though not much of a tourist attraction. From my eyes it’s the city that shimmers like a thousand splendid suns in the summer, rains like Augustus fell in love with Hazel Grace in Fault in our Stars; first slowly then all at once and the winters that wrap you up in theevening like a hug from a dear one, cold at first but then comforting.And best of all is my Alma Mater, NIT Raipur, sturdy building standing tall with its two wings as if welcoming everyone with open arms.
Just like any other city, Raipur is built from the rock, clay, bricks and cement, but Beautiful Raipur is made by a very special bunch of people. The people who made these four years look like the blink of an eye. As life feasted on the hopes, dreams and ambitions of me and mine, each part of this city became familiar to me with the tears and triumphs of my dear ones. I know where to go to cry and where to go to celebrate, the city, no longer bricks and mortar, smiles, cheers, motivates, and sheds tears with me. The smiles of loved ones, the food trails(ask me anything, I know best Gujrati to best gupchup), the triple ridesnarrowly avoiding traffic cops , the shopping, the festivals, the long silent walks, killing each other with sarcasms and slangs yet holding on to each other like a family. Be it the bakars with Aawazor the incessant hangouts withchota, bondo&jobu or the lessons from old man. Be it Chicken with Appoo, bunking classes with bhand, nukkadwith Sullsor the constant wragling with pallo sir &motabheem. From the Gup-chupwith sardooto Nukkadki chai with addu&jadu to the feel alive drives with abhisiree. With all these there always was Raipur one place at a time and that beholds the beauty of this city. All these and much more is what makeRaipur My Raipur. Raipur happened to me like a fairy tale and to call it only beautiful would be demeaning.
The sad part is that it doesn’t end here like a good movie....InsteadZindagiMigzara !!!
