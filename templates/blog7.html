#RaipurIsBeautiful

Beauty, they say, lies in the eyes of the beholder. My Raipur is beautiful, the kind of beauty that doesn't diminish everything around; rather makes everything else shine too. I have seen Raipur evolve in the last few years and have seen wide roads and big buildings develop in Naya Raipur which was very much just land; a short while back. But again, the beauty of MY Raipur is beyond concrete and mortar. Raipur, in a very short time, brought me people whom I will keep for life, to laugh with me or cry, to fight for me or reconcile and to be there no matter what. This beauty makes Raipur stand apart from other cities for me. Raipur is attractive in the smallest of food stalls for the biggest celebrations. Raipur is pretty at the lake side which offers balm to an aching soul. Raipur is breath-taking in the long walks with friends and bad singing. Raipur is dainty in the mall visits. Raipur is adorable in the new greens after monsoons. Raipur is heavenly in the first rays of warm sunlight on cold winter mornings. Raipur is alluring in the search of unknown roads on tripsies on two wheelers. Finally, Raipur is gorgeous in the company of my amazing friends. In closing, yes Raipur is beautiful, yet mere 'beautiful' doesn't seem to cover half of it. It is so much more...and yes all of us know it.
