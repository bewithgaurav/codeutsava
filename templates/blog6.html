#RaipurIsBeautiful

Raipur sheher me apka swagat hai! Most of us would have heard these lines as we land in Raipur. Being born here, my love for this city, couldn’t be described in words. A non-native person’s first reaction would be, Eh, where’s Raipur? Is it a town or a village? But believe me, this place is light years ahead!


First let me get the facts straight. Raipur is the fastest developing city and the capital of India’s fastest growing state, Chhattisgarh. It holds the pride to have established India’s premier institutes like NIT, IIT, IIIT, IIM, HNLU, AIIMS & GMC all within a 10 km radius! (Proud feeling!) Naya Raipur is the world’s first integrated city! And yeah, I am proud to say that it houses the World’s 4th largest cricket ground! Woah!


Enough boasting. Now you are in the city, probably experiencing a lot of heat! But that is nothing in front of the warmth of the people of this lovely city. So welcoming that they make you feel at home. And Raipur rains, Oh this city looks the most beautiful during rains!


Time to explore Raipur? Let’s head to Marine Drive. Popularly known as MD, this place is the favourite hangout spot of youngsters. And the cherry on the cake is that, it’s got FREE WIFI!!
Strolling in the cool breeze at MD, you can forgot all your life’s worries.


Feeling hungry? Let’s head to Jaisthamb chowk for the refreshing Poha or Rathod chowk for those hot and spicy Mangodis! And for the suave ones, there a lot of fast food chains like McD, KFC, Domino’s, Pizza Hut and malls to hangout and chill out!
Gol Bazaar is a bliss for street shoppers! Ghatarani and Jungle Safari are superb weekend getaways!


And since most of you reading this belong to our beloved NIT Raipur, believe me this place is going to give you the 4 most beautiful, amazing and transforming years of your life! Those triple rides, mass bunks, hanging out at Amul, college canteen, those late nights during college fests, each of this will become your most prized possession. You are going to make friends for a lifetime and relations that you will cherish forever!


t

So, #RaipurIsBeautiful and Raipur is not just a city, it’s a feeling!
