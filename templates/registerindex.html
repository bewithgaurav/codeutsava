FAQs

  • How much it will cost?

    Zero

  • What's the application process like?

    The application process is online based. Teams will be shortlisted on the basis of previous experiences,projects etc.

  • How big can a team be?

    Team size is exactly 3 members per team

  • When does registration open?

    1st January 2017

  • What about Accommodation?

    We will make special arrangements for subsidized accommodation in case you're arriving a day before the event

  • Who can attend?

    All undergraduate, graduate, and high school students are welcome. If you are under 18, we’ll need a parental consent form.

  • Once I get accepted,what do I need to bring?

    Your valid student ID, laptop, phone, chargers, extention cords, any sleeping equipment you might want (we'll be providing mattresses).

  • What do I get by attending CodeUtsava?

    Exposure to leading tech-companies,internship offers and Knowledge about recent technologies and prizes worth 2,00,000

  • What is the last date to register?

    19th Jan 2017

  • What if this FAQ didn't answer my questions?

    Feel free to drop us a message at our facebook page. We will get back to you soon.

CODEUTSAVA 1.0

CodeUtsava 1.0 is a mega computer science event and will span for two days: 4th and 5th Feb, 2017. The event will consist of a 28-hour hackathon that will run in parallel to Guest Lecture sessions. The event aims at encouraging the use of newer and advanced technologies and to promote better and cleaner ways of software and product development.
